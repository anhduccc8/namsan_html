ABOUT

If you can imagine it we can create it.

Our story began with a view through a window. In 2002, we expanded that vision to define our future — shifting from setting the scene to creating the characters. With over 40 years of proven industry experience in retail, wholesale, visual merchandising and concept development, it empowers us to create each mannequin with a distinctive charisma, because we understand each brand needs its own character.
OUR PROCESS

We can design, develop and produce props in all sizes and quantities.

Bring props to life for clients like Moncler and others. Any shape or design if suitable for the purpose. For retail solutions or within your shop window scheme... even a keychain will do its job.
